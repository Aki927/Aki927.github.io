My Journey

My career journey began with a degree in Human Resources from Simon Fraser University. I started as an HR assistant, honing my skills in people management and organizational development. My path then led me to the telecom industry, where I worked as an account executive, focusing on business development and forging meaningful client relationships. During the pandemic lockdown, my innate curiosity drove me to teach myself how to code. This newfound passion for technology led to my acceptance into the Bachelor of Computing Science program at Thompson Rivers University, marking a pivotal shift in my career towards software engineering.

Beyond my professional life, I'm an introvert with an INTJ-T personality, characterized by a strong desire to learn and a deep interest in the STEM field. Embracing a vegan lifestyle for the past two years has been a fulfilling journey, complemented by my love for travel, which I often embark on with my partner. Our adventures are always accompanied by our two beloved dogs, Shio and Pepper. Whether it's exploring new places, diving into the latest tech trends, or enjoying quiet moments at home, I constantly seek new experiences and knowledge to enrich my life. Let's connect and share our journeys!
